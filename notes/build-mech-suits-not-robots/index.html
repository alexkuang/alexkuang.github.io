build mech suits, not robots

General philosophy of software / automation design. Rather than build a "robot" that entirely replaces a human, build a "mech suit" that lets the human do human things. e.g. mech suit allows human to move fast + lift heavy things, human decides what heavy things most need lifting where.

Especially relevant in the age of LLMs because AI can't make subjective value decisions.
